We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you use our website and services. This may include:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and postal address

We may also automatically collect certain information when you use our website and services, including:

  • IP address and device information

  • How We Use Your Personal Information

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our website and services

  • To communicate with you about your orders and transactions

  • To conduct research and analysis to improve our business and services
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements

We may also use your personal information for marketing purposes, such as sending you promotional emails and messages. You can opt-out of receiving these communications at any time by following the instructions in the email or message.

We may disclose your personal information to the following parties:

How We Protect Your Personal Information

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure. This includes using encryption and secure connections for sensitive information, restricting access to personal information, and regularly reviewing our security practices.

However, no security measures are 100% effective, and we cannot guarantee the security of your personal information.

  • The right to request the deletion of your personal information

  • The right to withdraw your consent to the processing of your personal information

Please note that some of these rights may be subject to legal and regulatory restrictions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated Privacy Policy on our website and indicating the date of the last update.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or our practices regarding your personal information, please contact us at support@roovo.in.
