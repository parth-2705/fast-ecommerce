  1. Under the Terms & Conditions that Sellers agree upon, products are returnable within the return window if a user receives a product that is physically damaged, has missing parts or accessories, is defective or different from their description on the product detail page. All products listed under a particular category may have a different returns policy. Users are required to check & verify the return policy before ordering a product.
  2. Certain items are non-returnable as per the terms stipulated under Annexure A (refer to the table provided at the end of this Policy) of this Policy. Any attempt to return such items will not be accepted and no refund or exchange will be provided.
  3. Roovo is only responsible for arranging to troubleshoot the users' complaints and directing the users to the Sellers. The ultimate responsibility for the replacement or refund lies with the Sellers.
  4. Return will only be processed if:
    1. It is determined that the product was not damaged while in your possession;
    2. The product is not different from what was shipped to you; and
    3. The product is returned in the condition that it was received including the original box, MRP tag, user manual, warranty card, and all accessories)
  1. In case a user wants to return an electronic device that stores personal information, the user himself/herself shall be responsible for ensuring that all personal information stored in the device is deleted and the device is returned to factory settings. Roovo will not be liable in any manner for any misuse or usage of such information.
  2. For all products, the returns/replacement policy provided on the product page (if a product-specific policy exists) shall prevail over the general returns policy. Else the general returns policy shall be applicable. Do refer to the respective item's applicable return/replacement policy on the product page for any exceptions to this returns policy. In certain cases, a product may be ‘non-returnable’. In such cases, the product page will specifically highlight this and the user cannot claim a return/replacement at a later stage.
  3. In case the Seller is unable to provide a replacement for the product, in that case, a refund will be provided.
  4. During a return/replacement pickup, the product will be checked for the following:
    1. Correct & complete product: The same IMEI/Name/Brand/Serial number should be clearly visible along with all the accessories, etc..
    2. The product should be unused, unwashed, unsoiled, without any stains, and with non-tampered quality checks/warranty seals (wherever applicable)
    3. The product and packaging should not be damaged and should be in the condition that it was received.
    4. The pickup executive is free to refuse to accept the product for pick-up in case the above conditions are not met.
